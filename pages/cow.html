Cow evolves, builds fire

Minneapolis, reported by Patty Soup

It isn't often that we get the opportunity to see evolution in action. Today that changed; a rural Minnesota farmer reported that he saw one of his milk cows intentionally starting a fire today.

"I been dairy farming a long time, but I ain't never seen nothing like this before, and I betcha yous guys ain't never seen nothing like it, either," said Andrew Notkottson. "I walked into the barn yesterday morning and seen my Bessie standing in front of a campfire. She had one of them fireplace lighters tied to her hoof. Pretty smart for a cow without hands!"

It is still unclear how the cow tied the lighter to her hoof, but local experts at the University of Alabama agree that it is an unmistakeable sign of evolution occurring before our eyes.
