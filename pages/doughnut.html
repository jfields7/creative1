Texas announces construction of world's largest doughnut factory

Dallas, reported by Kelly Brisket

Lt. Governor Buck Eclair announced that the Texas state government is partnering with Williams & Williams Contractors to build what will be the largest doughnut factory in the world. Details are forthcoming, but the results are expected to be delicious and completed by July 2021.
