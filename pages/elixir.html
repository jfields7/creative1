Queen Elizabeth finds Elixir of Life

London, reported by Nigel Biscuit

God save the queen! Her Royal Majesty Queen Elizabeth II is reported to have found the famed Philosopher's Stone. While she has little need of its legendary (and apparently inaccurate) ability to transmute lead into gold, it has granted the Queen eternal life through its mystical elixir. Consequently, she has announced that she has no intention of stepping down from the throne despite spending more than sixty years as a monarch.

"I haven't felt this well in decades! I may tire of the crown one day, but it would be silly not to continue serving the United Kingdom with all this newfound vigor," she said.

Political opponents of the crown are reported to be quite devastated; many assumed that their best opportunity to abolish the monarchy would come with the aging ruler's passing. However, public approval of the Royal Family surged after the Queen's announcement.
