Can we trust him?

New York City, reported by Fred Hershey

"BACON!"

Jim Gaffigan's famous skit about bacon may be more than just a good gag. Despite his family-friendly persona and lovable demeanor, local reports suggest that Gaffigan may, in fact, be concocting a plan to steal vast quantities of bacon.

Using his own theory that one could travel in time by putting bacon bits on bacon, Gaffigan could potentially steal the delicious strips of cured pork by sneaking through time. It is unclear quite how much bacon he could steal, but a neighbor (who asked to remain anonymous) claims to have seen the comedian carrying an entire wagon of bacon to his New York City apartment on multiple occasions.

"It's more bacon than you can find in a supermarket, so he must be getting it from somewhere! I keep hearing weird noises from his apartment. It sounds like a vacuum cleaner, but I'm pretty sure he's up to something."

To verify these claims, fearless COW News reporter Fred Hershey contacted Gaffigan.

"That's ridiculous," he said. "My wife and I have five kids. It takes a whole wagonload just to feed them for breakfast!"

When asked about his sources, Gaffigan replied that he shops at Costco. The manager for the Manhattan Costco was unavailable for comment, but local employees have reported seeing an overweight man in sneakers buying entire crates of bacon. It is unclear if it is Gaffigan or one of the other 1.6 million residents of Manhattan.
